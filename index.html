Engagement Analysis in ADHD students

Human Machine Interaction(HMI) Lab, IIIT Delhi.
Advised by: Dr. Jainendra Shukla
1 in 10 children between ages 5 to 17 years receives an ADHD diagnosis, making this one of the most common childhood neurodevelopmental disorders in the World.
My work is to identify attention correlates and predicting the level of attention in students with ADHD using Machine Learning and Deep Learning. The modality used is GSR(Galvanic Skin Response) and HRV(Heart Rate Variability) Signals.
